"Roll. Imagine. Tell your story."

Tellio is a creative family dice game where rolled dice show images — and you bring them to life your way. With 9 unique themes and 7 curated image sets, no two games are ever the same. Add players, track stars, discover who earns the most across all rounds. Create custom sets from your own photos and make the game entirely personal. A full results history shows every game ever played.

Fair & fun rating: after each turn, players rate the storyteller 1–5 stars for artistry, creativity, and wit. In Hidden mode, stars are calculated automatically based on how well others guessed.
