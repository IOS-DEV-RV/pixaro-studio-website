Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2026

1. General Provisions

This Privacy Policy explains how the Tellio mobile application (the "Application") handles privacy. Tellio is designed to work without collecting personal data from users.

2. What the Application Does

Tellio is a creative dice storytelling game. The Application allows you to roll image dice, play themed storytelling rounds, track scores, create custom dice sets, and view game history.

3. No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, sell, share, or transfer personal data from users of the Application.

The Application does not require account registration and does not ask you to provide your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, or other personal information.

4. Local-Only App Data

Any information created inside the Application is stored locally on your device only. This may include:

  • Player names entered for gameplay
  • Game scores and session history
  • Custom dice set names
  • Images selected for custom dice sets
  • Application preferences

This local data is not uploaded to our servers and is not accessible to us.

5. Photo Library Access

Tellio may request access to your Photo Library only when you choose to add your own images to custom dice sets.

Selected photos are used only inside the Application and remain on your device. We do not upload, collect, analyze, or share your photos.

6. Location, Contacts, and Sensitive Data

The Application does not collect precise location data, contacts, health data, financial information, browsing history, or other sensitive personal data.

7. Payments and Subscriptions

The Application may offer optional premium features through Apple In-App Purchase.

All payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive, collect, or store payment card details, billing addresses, or other payment information.

You can manage subscriptions in iOS Settings. Deleting the Application does not automatically cancel an active subscription.

8. Children's Privacy

Tellio does not knowingly collect personal data from children. The Application is designed so that gameplay data stays on the device.

9. Data Sharing

Because we do not collect personal data from users, we do not sell, rent, share, or disclose such data to third parties.

10. Data Deletion

Since Tellio data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it by removing the Application from your device. You can also manage app permissions, including Photo Library access, in iOS Settings.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be published on this page.
