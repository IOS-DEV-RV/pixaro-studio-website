Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 24, 2026

1. General Provisions

This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected or processed in the Tellio mobile application (hereinafter referred to as the "Application"), available on iPhone. By using the Application, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. What the Application Does

Tellio is a creative dice storytelling game. The Application allows you to roll dice with images, play themed storytelling rounds, track player scores, create custom dice sets using your own photos, and view game history.

3. Data Collection

3.1 Data Necessary for Functionality

The Application may request access to:

  • Photo Library — to let you select images when creating custom dice sets. Access is requested via the system dialog and can be revoked at any time in Settings.

Your photos are used solely to build your custom sets. They are processed entirely on your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

3.2 User-Provided Information

You may voluntarily provide:

  • Player names entered within the Application
  • Custom dice set names and content
  • Messages to the support service
  • Feedback

3.3 Automatically Collected Information

The Application may automatically collect limited technical information:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Application usage statistics
  • Crash and error data

This information is used exclusively to maintain and improve the Application.

4. Local Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device:

  • Player names and game history
  • Custom dice sets and associated photos
  • Scores and session results
  • Application preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

5. Use of Analytics Services

We use Mixpanel, a third-party analytics service, to analyze feature usage, improve user experience, diagnose errors, and improve stability. Mixpanel receives anonymized event data that does not identify you personally. Learn more at mixpanel.com/legal/privacy-policy.

6. Use of Information

We use collected information to provide and maintain the Application's features, support users, improve quality and performance, ensure security, and fulfill legal obligations.

7. Data Transfer and Disclosure

We do not sell or transfer your personal data to third parties. Transfer is possible only to service providers necessary for the Application's operation, authorities when required by law, or in cases necessary to protect rights, safety, or property.

8. Location Information

The Application does not collect precise real-time location data. We may approximately determine the user's region based on IP address, device language, and App Store region — solely for improving the Application.

9. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. Since all game data and photos are stored locally on your device, the risk of unauthorized access to your content is significantly reduced.

10. Children

The Application is suitable for users aged 3 and older. However, we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data, please contact us.

11. User Rights (GDPR, CCPA)

Depending on the legislation of your country, you may have the right to request access, correct, delete, restrict processing, or receive a portable copy of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the address below.

12. Subscriptions and Payments

The Application offers premium features via subscription, including access to all dice sets, additional game modes, and unlimited custom sets.

All payments are processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not have access to your payment card data, do not store any payment information, and receive only anonymized confirmation of a completed purchase.

Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage your subscription at: Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions. Deleting the Application does not cancel an active subscription.

13. Changes to the Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The updated version will be published within the Application. Continued use of the Application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
