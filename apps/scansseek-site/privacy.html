Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 16, 2026  ·  ScanSeek

1 General Provisions

This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected or processed in the ScanSeek mobile application (hereinafter referred to as the "Application"), available on iPhone, iPad, and other platforms, as well as on related websites.

By using the Application, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2 Processing of Images and Documents

The Application is designed for scanning documents, converting images to PDF, and text search.

Local Processing:

  • All images are processed locally on the user's device
  • We do not upload photos or documents to our servers
  • We do not have access to the content of your documents
  • Images are used solely to perform the Application's functions

3 Data Collection

3.1 Data Necessary for Functionality

The Application may use:

  • The device's camera for scanning documents
  • Photos from the user's gallery
  • Files selected by the user

This data is processed only on the user's device and is not transmitted to us.

3.2 User-Provided Information

You may voluntarily provide information, including:

  • Data related to purchases and subscriptions
  • Messages to support service
  • Feedback

3.3 Automatically Collected Information

The Application may automatically collect limited technical information:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Unique device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Usage statistics and crash data

This information is used exclusively to ensure the Application's operation and improvement.

4 Use of Analytics Services

We may use third-party analytics services for:

  • Analyzing Application usage
  • Improving features and enhancing stability
  • Error diagnostics

Such services receive anonymized data only.

5 Use of Information

We use information for:

  • Providing the Application's features
  • User support and improving quality of work
  • Ensuring security
  • Fulfilling legal obligations

6 Data Transfer and Disclosure

We do not sell or transfer your personal data to third parties. Transfer is possible only to:

  • Service providers (e.g., analytics)
  • Comply with legal requirements
  • Protect rights, safety, and property

7 Data Storage

We do not store on our servers:

  • Scanned images
  • User documents
  • Text extracted from documents

If the user saves files, they are stored only on their device or in a cloud service of their choice.

8 Location Information

The Application does not collect precise real-time location data. We may approximately determine the user's region based on IP address, device settings, or App Store region. This is necessary for the proper functioning of the Application.

9 Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. Since images are processed locally and are not stored on servers, the risk of unauthorized access is significantly reduced.

10 Children

The Application is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or other age as established by the laws of the user's country of residence). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

11 User Rights (GDPR, CCPA, and other laws)

Depending on the legislation of your country, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Correct or delete your data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Receive a copy of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

12 Subscriptions and Payments

The Application may offer paid features via subscription.

Payment Processing: All payments are processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not have access to your bank card data, do not store payment information, and only receive anonymized purchase details.

Automatic Renewal: Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Payment will be charged to your Apple ID account.

Managing Your Subscription: Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. Deleting the Application does not cancel the subscription.

13 Changes to the Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The updated version will be published in the Application or on the website.

14 Your Consent

By using the Application, you confirm your consent to this Privacy Policy.
