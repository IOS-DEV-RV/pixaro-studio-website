Privacy Policy

Detectra  ·  Effective Date: May 12, 2026

1. General Provisions

This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected or processed in the Detectra mobile application (hereinafter — the "Application"), available on iPhone. By using the Application, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. What is Detectra

Detectra is a privacy detection tool for iPhone. The Application uses three detection modes — a lens detector powered by the iPhone camera with color filters, a magnetic field scanner using the built-in magnetometer, and an acoustic analyzer using the microphone — to help users identify potential hidden surveillance devices in their surroundings. Detectra is a personal awareness tool. It does not claim to replace professional RF sweeping or counter-surveillance equipment.

3. Data Collection

3.1 Data Required for Functionality

The Application may request access to:

  • Camera — used by the Lens Detector mode to apply real-time color filters that help visually identify reflective lens surfaces. The live camera feed is processed locally on your device and is never uploaded to any server. Photos captured during a detection session are stored only on your device.
  • Microphone — used by the Acoustic Analyzer mode to measure ambient sound and analyze the high-frequency spectrum (10–18 kHz range). Audio is processed locally in real time and is never recorded or transmitted to any server.
  • Location — used to tag each saved detection record with GPS coordinates, a reverse-geocoded street address, and a static map preview. Location is stored locally on your device and is never sent to our servers.
  • Motion / Magnetometer — used by the Magnetic Field Scanner mode to read electromagnetic field strength from the built-in magnetometer. Sensor data is processed locally on your device.

All permissions are requested via the system dialog and can be revoked at any time in iOS Settings.

3.2 User-Provided Information

You may voluntarily provide:

  • Detection record notes and descriptions
  • Messages sent to our support team

3.3 Automatically Collected Information

The Application may automatically collect limited technical information:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Application usage statistics
  • Crash reports and error data

This information is used exclusively to maintain and improve the Application.

4. Local Data Storage

All application data is stored locally on your device using CoreData and the local file system:

  • Detection records (type, timestamp, sensor readings at time of capture)
  • Captured photos from the Lens Detector mode
  • GPS coordinates and reverse-geocoded addresses
  • Static map preview images for each location
  • Application settings and preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

5. Use of Analytics Services

We use Mixpanel, a third-party analytics service, to:

  • Analyze feature usage within the Application
  • Improve the user experience
  • Diagnose errors and crashes
  • Improve stability and performance

Mixpanel receives anonymized event data that does not personally identify you and never includes camera frames, audio recordings, sensor readings, or precise location. You can learn more at mixpanel.com/legal/privacy-policy.

6. Subscription Management

Premium features are unlocked via subscription and managed using Superwall, a paywall and subscription orchestration service. Superwall receives anonymized subscription events (such as which paywall was shown and which plan was selected) but does not have access to your camera feed, audio, sensor data, captured photos, or location. You can learn more at superwall.com/privacy.

7. Use of Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Application's detection features
  • Support users and respond to inquiries
  • Improve the quality and performance of the Application
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Fulfill legal obligations

8. Data Transfer and Disclosure

We do not sell or transfer your personal data to third parties. Transfer is possible only to:

  • Service providers necessary for the Application's operation (analytics, paywall orchestration)
  • Authorities, when required by applicable law
  • In cases necessary to protect rights, safety, or property

9. Location Information

The Application uses location data only to tag detection records with GPS coordinates and to render a static map preview for each record. Location is stored locally on your device and is never sent to our servers. We may approximately determine the user's region based on IP address or device region settings solely for improving the Application's performance.

10. Camera and Microphone Data

Camera frames and microphone audio are processed entirely on-device in real time. No camera footage or audio is recorded, stored as a media file, or transmitted to any server. Photos captured by the user within the Lens Detector are stored locally on-device only.

11. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. Since all detection data, photos, and location records are stored locally on your device and we do not store them on our servers, the risk of unauthorized access to your content is significantly reduced.

12. Children
