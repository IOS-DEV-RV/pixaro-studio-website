Privacy Policy

Word Cascade Game · Effective Date: May 19, 2026

1. General Provisions

This Privacy Policy applies to all information processed in the Word Cascade Game mobile application (the "Application"), available on iPhone. By using the Application, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. What the Application Does

Word Cascade Game is a social word explanation game. Players split into teams, explain words under changing rules, track round results, and continue sessions directly on one device.

3. Data Collection

The Application may process the following categories of data:

  • User-provided data: player names, round settings, selected categories, and support messages.
  • Gameplay data: scores, rounds, streaks, and session history saved for continuity.
  • Technical data: device model, OS version, app version, crash logs, and anonymous diagnostics.

Core gameplay data is stored locally on your device and is not sold to third parties.

4. Local Storage

Word Cascade Game stores gameplay content locally on device to preserve your sessions and preferences, including:

  • team setup and round configuration
  • session scores and local progress
  • in-app experience settings
