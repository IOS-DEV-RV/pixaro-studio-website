Privacy Policy

Collaglow · Effective Date: June 1, 2026

1. General Provisions

This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected or processed in the Collaglow mobile application (the "Application"), available on iPhone. By using the Application, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. What is Collaglow

Collaglow is a shape collage and photo grid editor that helps users create polished collages using templates, adjustable layouts, photo filters, text overlays, emoji, photo overlays, canvas backgrounds and export tools.

3. Data Required for Functionality

The Application may request access to the following system features:

  • Photo Library — used to import photos into collage sections, overlay images, and export finished collages back to your library when you choose to save or share.
  • Notifications — used only if you enable optional reminders or app-related alerts in the future. Notifications are scheduled locally on your device when applicable.

You can manage these permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

4. Local Storage

Most Collaglow content is stored locally on your device, including collage layouts, photo slot states, saved collage history, text styles, overlay positions, filter presets and editor preferences. This helps keep your work available inside the Application without requiring an account.

5. Analytics

We use Mixpanel to understand feature usage, improve product stability and detect technical issues. Mixpanel receives event-level analytics data that does not directly identify you. Learn more at mixpanel.com/legal/privacy-policy.

6. Subscription Services

The Application uses Superwall for paywall presentation and subscription flow orchestration. Superwall may process anonymous paywall and purchase-flow events. Learn more at superwall.com/privacy.

7. Payments

Payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details. Subscription renewal and cancellation are managed in iOS Settings.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared only with service providers required for Application functionality, analytics, diagnostics or when legally required.

9. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organisational safeguards to protect information handled by the Application. No digital system can be guaranteed completely secure, but we limit processing to what is needed for the product experience.

10. Children

The Application is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal data, contact us for review.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, delete or restrict processing of your data. You may also request additional details via the contact email below.

12. Policy Updates
