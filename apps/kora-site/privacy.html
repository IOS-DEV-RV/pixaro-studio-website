Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Welcome to Kora — your daily habit and productivity companion. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to provide and improve the app experience:

  • Usage analytics: We use Mixpanel to collect anonymized, non-personally identifiable data about how you interact with the app — such as which features you use and how frequently. This helps us understand what works best and improve future updates. No personally identifiable information is sent to Mixpanel.
  • Subscription data: If you subscribe to Kora Premium, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple via the App Store and StoreKit. We do not collect, store, or process your payment or billing information directly.
  • Notification permissions: Kora may request permission to send local push notifications (e.g., Pomodoro timer completion, habit reminders). These notifications are generated entirely on-device and are never sent through our servers.
  • Support communications: If you contact us via the in-app support form or by email, we receive your email address and the content of your message solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not share this data with any third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core app functionality including habit tracking, Pomodoro timer, Eisenhower Matrix, countdown reminders, and daily wisdom.
  • To process and validate your subscription status via Apple's StoreKit framework.
  • To analyze anonymized usage patterns using Mixpanel and improve our features and user experience.
  • To deliver local push notifications for timer events and scheduled reminders you have configured.
  • To display contextual paywall screens using the Superwall SDK.
  • To respond to support requests submitted through the app or by email.

3. Data Storage

All your personal data — habits, goals, notes, Eisenhower Matrix tasks, achievements, and app preferences — is stored exclusively on your device using iOS standard local storage (CoreData and UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device and is not uploaded to any server.

If you have iCloud backup enabled on your iPhone, iOS may back up this local data to iCloud as part of the standard system backup. This is governed entirely by Apple's Privacy Policy and is outside our control.

4. Third-Party Services

Kora integrates the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Mixpanel — anonymized usage analytics. Mixpanel Privacy Policy
  • Superwall — in-app paywall and subscription screen management. Superwall Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / StoreKit — in-app purchases and subscription management. Apple Privacy Policy

5. Permissions We Request

  • Notifications: Used to deliver Pomodoro timer completion alerts and habit reminders you have set up. You can manage or disable these at any time from iPhone Settings → Kora → Notifications.

We do not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, photo library, or any other sensitive system resource unless explicitly required for a specific feature you choose to use.

You may revoke any granted permissions at any time from iPhone Settings → Kora.

6. Children's Privacy

Kora is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

Since all personal content (habits, tasks, notes, preferences) is stored locally on your device, you retain full control over it at all times. You may delete any or all data directly within the app or by uninstalling Kora from your device.

Depending on your location, you may also have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any information we hold (such as analytics data or support emails). To exercise these rights, please contact us at admin@terasms.ru.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we handle. All analytics data sent to Mixpanel is anonymized and transmitted over encrypted connections. We do not store any sensitive personal data on our own servers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or applicable law. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of Kora after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

Email: admin@terasms.ru
