What Kora Can Do

Everything you need.
Every single day.

01

Your Day at a Glance

The Home screen shows today's date, a week strip, and your full daily plan split into Goals and Notes. Quick-access buttons bring you to Matrix, Pomodoro, or Countdown in one tap.

  • Weekly date strip with current day highlight
  • Goals and Notes tabs in one list
  • Quick launch for Matrix, Pomodoro, Countdown

02

Monthly Calendar & Habit List

Tap any day in the monthly calendar to see that day's habits and goals. Each day shows a circular progress ring. Swipe between months and check completion at a glance — all in one fluid sheet.

  • Full month calendar with progress rings per day
  • Tap any day to view its Goals or Notes
  • Completion count shown for the selected date

03

Pomodoro Focus Timer

A clean circular timer with customizable Focus and Break durations. Switch between Focus Session and Rest with one tap. The session counter tracks your completed rounds, and you get notified the moment time is up — even if the app is closed.

  • Adjustable Focus (default 35 min) and Break (5 min)
  • Sessions completed counter, resets daily
  • Background push notification on timer end

04

Eisenhower Matrix

Four quadrants — Do Now, Schedule, Delegate, and Eliminate — help you sort every task by urgency and importance. Tap the + button on any quadrant to add a task there instantly. Always know what deserves your attention first.

  • Do Now · Schedule · Delegate · Eliminate
  • Add tasks directly to any quadrant
  • Color-coded by urgency and importance

05

Wisdom of the Day

Each day a new inspirational quote is waiting for you. Scratch the card to reveal it — then save it to your personal Collection if it resonates. Future days are locked until they arrive, keeping the ritual fresh and meaningful.

  • New quote every day — scratch to reveal
  • Save favorites to your Wisdom Collection
  • Future quotes locked, anticipation builds

06

Create Habits Your Way

Add a Note, a Cultivate habit (positive things to grow), or a Release habit (things to let go of). Pick an icon from the library, choose a color, set a Daily Reminder, and define a repeat schedule — every day, certain days, by interval, or fully custom.

  • Note · Cultivate · Release habit types
  • Custom icon, color, and reminder
  • Flexible repeat: daily, custom days, interval
