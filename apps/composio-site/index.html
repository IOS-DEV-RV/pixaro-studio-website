How Composio Rates Ingredients

Three colors.
One quick decision.

Safe

Common, well-studied ingredients with no known concerns at typical food doses.

Use with Care

Acceptable but worth limiting — added sugars, sweeteners, dyes, and similar additives.

Avoid

Substances linked to health concerns or banned in some regions — Composio flags them clearly.
