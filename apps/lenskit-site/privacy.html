Privacy Policy

Lens Kit Pro · Effective Date: March 24, 2026

1. General Provisions

This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected or processed in the Lens Kit Pro mobile application (hereinafter referred to as the "Application"), available on iPhone and other platforms, as well as on related websites (if any).

By using the Application, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Processing of Photos and Videos

The Application is designed for shooting photos and videos with manual camera controls, applying cinematic presets and filters, and editing media.

Local Processing

  • All photos, videos, and filter processing are performed locally on the user's device
  • We do not upload your photos or videos to our servers
  • We do not have access to the content of your media files
  • Images and videos are used solely to perform the Application's functions

3. Data Collection

3.1 Data Necessary for Functionality

The Application may use:

  • The device's camera for capturing photos and videos
  • The device's microphone for recording audio during video capture
  • Photos and videos from the user's gallery (for applying presets and editing)
  • Files selected by the user

This data is processed only on the user's device and is not transmitted to us.

3.2 User-Provided Information

You may voluntarily provide information, including:

  • Data related to purchases and subscriptions
  • Messages to the support service
  • Feedback

3.3 Automatically Collected Information

The Application may automatically collect limited technical information:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Usage statistics
  • Crash data

This information is used exclusively to ensure the Application's operation and improvement.

4. Use of Analytics Services

We may use third-party analytics services for:

  • Analyzing Application usage
  • Improving features and presets
  • Error diagnostics
  • Enhancing stability

Such services receive anonymized data.

5. Use of Information

We use information for:

  • Providing the Application's features
  • User support
  • Improving the quality and performance of the Application
  • Ensuring security
  • Fulfilling legal obligations

6. Data Transfer and Disclosure

We do not sell or transfer your personal data to third parties.

Transfer is possible only to:

  • Service providers (e.g., analytics)
  • Comply with legal requirements
  • Protect rights, safety, and property

7. Data Storage

We do not store on our servers:

  • Photos and videos captured in the Application
  • User media files
  • Custom presets or filter settings created by the user

If the user saves files, they are stored only on their device or in a cloud service of their choice (e.g., iCloud).

8. Location Information

The Application does not collect precise real-time location data.

We may approximately determine the user's region based on:

  • IP address
  • Device settings
  • App Store region

This is necessary for the proper functioning of the Application.

9. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. Since photos and videos are processed locally and are not stored on our servers, the risk of unauthorized access to your media is significantly reduced.

10. Children

The Application is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or other age as established by the laws of the user's country of residence).

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Depending on the legislation of your country, you may have the right to:

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

12. Subscriptions and Payments

The Application may offer paid features via subscription.

Payment Processing

All payments are processed by Apple via the App Store. We:

  • Do not have access to your bank card data
  • Do not store payment information
  • Only receive anonymized purchase details

Payment processing is carried out in accordance with Apple's privacy policy.

Automatic Renewal

Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Payment will be charged to your Apple ID account.

Managing Your Subscription

The user can manage their subscription and turn off auto-renewal in their Apple ID account settings:

  • Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions

Deleting the Application does not cancel the subscription.
