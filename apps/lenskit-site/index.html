Lens Kit Pro — Professional Manual Camera
with RAW & ProRes

Take full control of your iPhone's camera. Shoot in RAW for photos and ProRes for video. Adjust every setting manually and create your own presets for any lighting situation.

⚙️

Total manual control

  • ISO, Shutter Speed, White Balance, Exposure
  • Real-time parameter adjustment
  • Focus peaking and zebras
  • Professional histogram
🎬

Professional video mode

  • Record in Apple ProRes up to 4K
  • Cinema-quality footage straight out of camera
🎨

Create & apply custom presets

  • Save your favorite settings as presets
  • Apply filters and LUTs in real time
📐

Perfect composition every time

  • Customizable grids: Rule of thirds, golden ratio
  • Crop and reframe photos without quality loss
  • Level gauge for horizon control

Why Lens Kit Pro?

Your iPhone already has a great camera. We give you the keys to unlock its full potential. No more guessing — just pure creative control.
